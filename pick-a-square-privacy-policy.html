Pick-A-Square Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 9, 2026

Pick-A-Square is a square selection and board organization app. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Summary: Pick-A-Square does not require an account, does not process payments, does not collect location data, and does not sell personal information.

Information You Enter

The app may allow you to enter names, board labels, dates, and square selections so you can create and manage a Pick-A-Square board.

This information is used only to display and save your board inside the app or on your device. Pick-A-Square is not designed to send this information to us.

Local Storage

Pick-A-Square may save board information locally on your device using browser or app storage. This helps your boards remain available when you return to the app.

You can remove locally saved information by clearing the app/browser data or deleting saved boards inside the app where that option is available.

Information We Do Not Collect

Pick-A-Square does not intentionally collect:

  • Payment information
  • Precise location
  • Contacts
  • Photos, videos, or microphone recordings
  • Health or fitness information
  • Government identification information
  • Account passwords

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We do not share board information with advertisers or data brokers.

Information may be processed by services needed to operate the app or website, such as hosting providers, app store services, or the app wrapper used to make the website available as a mobile app. Those services may process technical information according to their own policies.

Children's Privacy

Pick-A-Square is not intended to collect personal information from children. If a parent or guardian believes that a child has provided personal information through the app, they should clear the saved app data from the device.

Security

Pick-A-Square is designed to keep board information on the user's device. No method of electronic storage or transmission is perfect, so users should avoid entering sensitive personal information into board squares or labels.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app changes or if legal, platform, or store requirements change. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions about Pick-A-Square, please use the contact options provided on the app store listing or the website where the app is published.
